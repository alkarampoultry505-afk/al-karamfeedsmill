Biosecurity Guidelines

Maintaining strict biosecurity measures is essential to prevent disease outbreaks and ensure the profitability of your farm.

  • **Restricted Entry:** Implement a controlled entrance system (Foot Bath, Vehicle Disinfection, Visitor Logbook).
  • **Cleaning & Disinfection:** Perform a complete clean-out and disinfection weekly, especially of equipment and sheds.
  • **Rodent & Pest Control:** Use traps, baits, and sprays regularly to control rodents, flies, and other vectors.
  • **Staff Hygiene:** Mandate uniform use, thorough handwashing, and prevent sick staff from entering the farm.
  • **Sick Bird Management:** Immediately quarantine any bird showing signs of illness and initiate proper diagnostic and treatment protocols.
  • **Record Keeping:** Maintain detailed records of feed consumption, water intake, medicine usage, and daily mortality.
