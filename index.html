About Us – Al Karam Poultry Feeds Mill Khairpur

Al Karam Poultry Feeds Mill Khairpur is one of the most trusted and quality-driven poultry feed manufacturers in Sindh. Our mill operates with modern machinery, premium raw materials, and a highly experienced technical team to produce healthy and balanced chicken feed for poultry farms across the region.

Our mission is to support poultry farmers with high–nutrition, fast–growth, and disease–resistant feed formulas that ensure maximum performance and healthy birds.

Our Features

  • 100% laboratory-tested and quality–assured feed
  • Daily production of more than 200 bags
  • Modern grinding, mixing, and pelleting system
  • Balanced nutrition for Starter, Grower, Broiler & Finisher feeds
  • Formulas designed as per age & seasonal requirements
  • Supervised by expert nutritionists and poultry doctors
  • Guidance and support for farmers

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable, and consistently reliable feed that ensures rapid weight gain, healthy growth, and better production.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading poultry feed producers in Sindh and Pakistan by delivering world-class quality and performance.

What Makes Us Different?

  • Scientifically formulated nutrient balance
  • High digestibility and fast weight gain
  • Healthy and clean ingredients
  • Experienced quality control team
  • Premium and consistent feed performance
